Majority Of Global Consumers Frustrated With Disconnected Experiences, 72% Willing To Seek Out Alternatives:

A ground-breaking study carried out by MuleSoft on How Global Consumer continue to receive disconnected experiences from organizations and API-led integration deliver connected experiences and earn customer trust. This study reveals the statistical data about the number of frustrated and disconnected experience customers and the people who seek out new service providers to get a more personalized experience. The Fieldwork was taken online across industries like Public Sector, Banking, Insurance, Retail and Healthcare; the samples were collected from US, UK, Germany, France, Australia, Singapore and Japan. This study also explains the Coherence Economy – a new approach to customer engagement where multiple brands collaborate with partners to add value through a digital ecosystem.

Mulesoft the leading Integration platform provider for building application networks has come up with a brand new solution; Anypoint Service Mesh which helps companies to simply discover, manage and secure microservice. There are plenty of benefits that customers can avail just by extending Anypoint Platform to any microservice.
